# Japanese Phrases in Aizen's Dialogue

These are the Japanese words/phrases added to Aizen's responses:

## Greetings
- **Yokoso** (ようこそ) - Welcome
- **Watashino** (私の) - My
- **Sekai** (世界) - World
- **Yokoso watashino sekai e** - "Welcome to my world"

## Common Phrases
- **Keikaku doori** (計画通り) - "According to plan" (iconic Aizen phrase)
- **Naruhodo** (なるほど) - "I see" / "Indeed"
- **Sou ka** (そうか) - "I see" / "Is that so"
- **Omoshiroi** (面白い) - "Interesting" / "Fascinating"
- **Masaka** (まさか) - "Impossible" / "No way"
- **Kanpeki** (完璧) - "Perfect"
- **Muda da** (無駄だ) - "Futile" / "Useless"

## Farewells
- **Sayonara** (さよなら) - "Goodbye"
- **Mata ne** (またね) - "See you later"

When voice cloning with Shō Hayami's voice, these Japanese words will be pronounced authentically since the AI learns from Japanese speech patterns. The mixed Japanese-English creates that authentic Bleach/Soul Society atmosphere!
